First time? Here's what you need to know.
You'll fill in your app details once — they're saved in your browser so you never retype them. When you generate, you get two files:
redirect.html — a tiny file your web team uploads to your website once, at a URL you choose. This is the address you'll enter below.
qr.png — your QR code image, ready to use on anything.
After the first setup, future campaigns only need you to fill in campaign details and download a new QR. No more file uploads, ever.
