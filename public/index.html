Free & open source

One QR code
for both stores

Scan on iPhone — goes to the App Store.
Scan on Android — goes to Google Play.
No subscriptions. Runs on your machine. Works forever.

Download ↓ see how it works
npm install -g appqr
How it works
01 — once
Run setup
Paste your two store URLs. Get a redirect.html and your first QR image.
02 — once
Upload one file
Drop redirect.html anywhere on your existing website. Never touch it again.
03 — forever
Run campaigns
New campaign? Run one command. Get a new QR image. No upload needed.
04 — magic
Device detected
Phone scans QR, hits your site, detects device, lands on the right store.
Usage
terminal
# first time — generates redirect.html + qr.png
appqr setup \
  --ios "https://apps.apple.com/app/id123" \
  --android "https://play.google.com/store/..." \
  --url "https://myapp.com/go"
 
# every campaign after — just a new qr.png
appqr campaign \
  --url "https://myapp.com/go" \
  --ios-params "ct=instagram&pt=12345&mt=8" \
  --android-params "utm_source=instagram"
